About Me

Hello, my name is Nguyễn Đình Đan Phi, a Mobile Developer with over 1 year of experience in mobile app development. I specialize in using SwiftUI and UIKit to create smooth and user-friendly applications. I have experience integrating third-party login methods like Google and Facebook, and I work with the MVVM architecture and popular design patterns such as Singleton, Delegate,... to build scalable and maintainable code. With a passion for technology, my goal is to become a professional developer, always ready to take on new challenges and continuously improve to meet the growing demands of the mobile technology industry.
